About Pooshaz

Pooshaz is a luxury Pakistani fashion house specializing in refined hand embroidery and timeless design. With over 4 years of experience, Pooshaz proudly serves customers across Pakistan, offering carefully handcrafted pieces that embody elegance, heritage, and premium craftsmanship.

At Pooshaz, luxury is defined by detail. Each creation is thoughtfully designed using high-quality fabrics and intricate embroidery techniques, resulting in garments that feel graceful, sophisticated, and enduring.
